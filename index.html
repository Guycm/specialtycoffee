Places I've Visited for Specialty coffee


Phuket

sync coffee

Sync Coffee has developed its own unique blend of coffees, teas and chocolates. Their must-try signature drinks include Jelly Affogato, Red Carpet and Brown Dirty.
A choice of breakfast options, snacks and baked goods such as croissants, brownies and coconut cake are also available.
It's an ideal place where you can sip a cup of coffee
or enjoy an easy meal in a beautifully minimalist space.
Sync Coffee is situated along Rimhad Road, only steps away from the Kamala Seafood Restaurant.

campus coffee roasters

Old Town area of Phuket, Campus Coffee Roaster serves a wide range of coffees from espresso to cold brew,
using its own individual process and roasted coffee beans.
The menu includes a variety of quick and easy
recipes including sandwiches, wraps as well as desserts and locally made snacks. Open from late morning
until early evening, this coffee cafe is an ideal place to relax or get some work done over a cup of coffee

graph coffee

Graph coffee is located in Phuket Old Town. It’s a good place to work for a bit too, as they have decent wifi and tables.
Other than really good coffee, they also have amazing Canele
pastries which you have to try! They are super crunchy on the
outside and so soft in the inside. Graph is a cafe that originates from a place close to Bangkok and now they have several locations across Thailand

New York

Devocion

At this coffee shop with roots in Bogotá, the beauty of the
space is matched by the quality of the coffee, which comes exclusively from small farms in Colombia.
Fresh beans are brought directly to Brooklyn, where they're roasted on-site within a few days of arrival.
Take a seat on one of the leather sofas under the skylight or
at a table in front of the living wall, and enjoy a cappuccino with one of the homemade medialunas or croissants.

stumptown coffee

My visit to Stumptown Coffee Roasters in New York was a coffee lover’s dream come true. The coffee’s rich aroma and perfectly balanced flavor made it an instant favorite. The café’s minimalist yet inviting design created a comfortable setting to savor each sip.The staff’s passion for coffee was evident,and they provided
valuable recommendations. Stumptown Coffee Roasters is more than a café. it’s a haven for coffee enthusiasts seeking exceptional
quality and a warm atmosphere. Whether you’re in need of a morning pick-me-up or a relaxing afternoon, this café is a must-visit in New York.

birch coffee

this is a local business that roasts all of its coffee beans in small batches just across the East River in Long Island City, Queens. Varieties of single-origin coffee and espresso change by the season, and as an added bonus they sell Dough Donuts, baked and delivered fresh daily.
